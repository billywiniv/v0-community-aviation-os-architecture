Community Aviation is building the operating system for general aviation. A pilot's journey from first dream to checkride to lifelong proficiency, guided by the Learn-Do-Fly Training Standard and powered by a network of named experts whose knowledge you can trust. One platform that grows with you — learning what you need, connecting you to who can help, and remembering where you've been.
The pilot is at the center. The network surrounds them. The scheduler connects everyone. Flight training providers plug in for operations. Same app, progressive features.
